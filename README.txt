		--Cat's Frozen Addon version 2.6--

Cat's Frozen addon for BrutalDoom v22 contains :
- An improved freezedeath system
	- Each frozen monster has now 8 angle sprites
	- Frozen monsters' head can be broken 
	- New ice effect
	- Monsters that resurrect after beeing frozen are weakened
	- Monsters cannot be headshot/legshot... with freeze damages
- New Enemy : FrostBaron
- New Enemy : Cryocubus
- New Enemy : FrostBrain
- New Weapon : CryoCannon (Slot 9) is dropped by the Cryocubus
- New weapon : Cryo Rifle (Slot 6) has a chance to spawn instead of a Plasma Rifle
- New weapon : Freezer (Slot 7) has a chance to spawn instead of a BFG
- New weapon : Freezing Grenade (Slot 0) can be acquired in backpacks
- New weapon : SnowCaster device(Slot 0) can be acquired in backpacks
- New weapon : Modular Pistol (slot 2) has a chance to spawn instead of a Minigun/SMG
- New Ammo for shotguns and for the rocket launcher
- New item : FreezeBot™ (Slot 0) has a chance to spawn instead of a captured marine (and you also get one in backpacks)
- New inventory items : Traps and turrets can be acquired as rewards or next to backpacks.
- Scientist !
